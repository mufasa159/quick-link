Data Storage

All of your Q-links are stored locally in your browser storage . The stored data is synced to your browser profile, meaning if you're logged in to your browser account or have browser sync feature enabled, your Q-links will be available on all devices where it's synced. Otherwise, the data is only available on the device where it was created.


Storage Capacity

As of June 2024, you can only store a maximum of 8KB of data due to limitations of the browser storage API.


Data Deletion

Stored data is deleted when you perform any of the following actions:

  • clear your browser's cache and cookies
  • sign out of your browser account
  • disable sync feature
  • uninstall the extension
